Black Box Warning: Antidepressants increase the risk of suicidal thinking and behavior in young adults (18-24) with MDD and other psychiatric disorders. Closely monitor for clinical worsening, suicidality or unusual changes in behavior particularly during the initial 1-2 months and during periods of dosage adjustments. Short-term studies did not show an increase in the risk of suicidality with Antidepressants compared to placebo in adults beyond age 24; There was a reduction in risk with Antidepressants compared to placebo in adults aged 65 and older.
