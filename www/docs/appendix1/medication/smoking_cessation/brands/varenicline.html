Varenicline

Back

Brand name: Chantix

First-line medications:

  • Nicotine Patch
  • Nicotine Gum
  • Nicotine Lozenge
  • Buproprion
  • Combination Therapy

Effective combinations of medications are:

  • Long-term (>14 weeks) nicotine patch (18-24 weeks in trials)+ other Nicotine Replacement Therapy (NRT: gum, lozenge, spray) as needed for a minimum of 26 weeks (up to 52 weeks in trials). Use beyond 52 weeks should be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
  • Nicotine patch (10 weeks)+ nicotine inhaler (12-26 weeks)
  • Nicotine patch (10 weeks)+ bupropion SR (12 weeks)

Second-line medication:

  • Varenicline Should only be considered after first line medications have failed.
