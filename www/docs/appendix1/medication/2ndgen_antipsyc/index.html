Adverse Effects Pros and Cons Monitoring, Referrals and Warnings Black Box Warning

Use caution in patients at risk for aspiration pneumonia. Esophageal dysmotility and aspiration has been associated with antipsychotic use. Use caution in hot weather since these drugs may increase susceptibility to heat stroke. Use caution to avoid overheating and dehydration. Use lower doses and more gradual increases in the elderly, debilitated, renal and hepatic patients.
