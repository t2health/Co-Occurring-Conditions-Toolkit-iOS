Modafinil

Back

Brand name: Provigil

Adult Starting Dose (Max Per Day)

  • Initial stimulant dose in the mTBI patient = 100mg QAM
  • Increase in increments of 100mg using split daily doses
  • Max stimulant dose in the mTBI patient = 400mg per day

Advantages

  • Use as a stimulant for treating fatigue in mTBI patients

Disadvantages

  • Adverse Events: headache and asthesia
  • Reduce the dose to one-half that recommended in one with hepatic impairment

Pregnancy Category

  • Risk cannot be ruled out, but potential benefits may justify potential risk

Safety Margins

  • Methylphenidate and Modafinil contraindicated within 14 days following MAOI therapy
  • Contraindications with Methylphenidate are halogenated anesthetics; Do not administer on the day of surgery
  • Avoid concurrent use with alcohol
  • Avoid concurrent use of Methylphenidate with ephedra and yohimbe
