Pros and Cons

mTBI

Pro: May be useful for some of the behavioral symptoms

Con: During titration phase of treatment, may increase anxiety and fatigue

Headache

Pro: May be used by specialty care providers

Con: Evidence is insufficient to recommend for or against use

Acute Stress

Pro: No additional

Con: During titration phase of treatment, may increase anxiety, nightmares and fatigue

PTSD

Pro: Venlafaxine is an effective first line treatment

Con: During titration phase of Venlafaxine, may increase anxiety, nightmares and fatigue.
Duloxetine has not been studied.

Depression

Pro: Very effective first-line treatment

Con: During titration phase of treatment, may increase anxiety, nightmares and fatigue

Chronic Pain

Pro: May be beneficial for neuropathic pain

Con: When used in conjunction with Opioids, sexual side effects may be even more pronounced

Substance Use Disorder

Pro: Help with sobriety in instances where comorbid depressive symptoms are effectively targeted

Con: No additional
