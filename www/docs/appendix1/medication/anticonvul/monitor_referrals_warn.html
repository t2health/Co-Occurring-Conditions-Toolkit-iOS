Monitoring, Referrals and Warnings: Monitor for increased depression or suicidality after initiation.

Topiramate: Monitor for nephrolithiasis, glaucoma-like symptom or visual disturbances, sodium bicarbonate levels and ammonia level.

Valproic Acid: Monitor for hepatotoxicty, pancreatitis, thrombocytopenia, hyperammonemia, and valproic acid levels. Routinely monitor LFTs, CBCs, PT/PTT and serum ammonia levels.

Carbamazepine:Monitor for blood cell abnormalities, carbamazepine levels, LFTs, Na, Urinalysis, Iron, BUN and Lipids. Perform baseline and periodic eye exams.

oxcarbazepine: Monitor for hyponatremia.
