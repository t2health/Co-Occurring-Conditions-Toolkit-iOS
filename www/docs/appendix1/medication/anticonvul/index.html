Pros and Cons Monitoring, Referrals and Warnings Black Box Warning

Slow titration is required for all medications either beginning or discontinuing the medication. Development of a skin rash requires discontinuation. Anticonvulsants are preferred for mood stabilization over antipsychotics as they can augment antidepressants and help with mood stabilization. Fewer serious long-term effects are seen as compared to antipsychotics. Blood level monitoring may be useful in cases of suspected toxicity. Use with caution in renal and hepatic disorder.
