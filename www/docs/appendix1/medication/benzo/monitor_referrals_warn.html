Monitoring, Referrals and Warnings: Monitor heart rate, blood pressure, respiration, excess sedation and mental status, especially suicidality. Perform periodic monitoring of liver and kidney function, hematocrit and CBC. Patients should be advised that Benzodiazepine use may impair the ability to perform hazardous activities requiring mental alertness or physical coordination (e.g, operating machinery and driving a motor vehicle).
