Monitoring, Referrals and Warnings Black Box Warning

Success is enhanced by engagement in a comprehensive management program that includes psychosocial therapy. Naltrexone po or IM or acamprosate should be routinely considered when treating alcohol dependence with addiction counseling. Disulfiram should only be used when abstinence is the goal. Compliance improves when disulfiram administration is directly observed. Inform the patient to use caution when operating vehicles and hazardous machinery since disulfiram may cause sedation.
