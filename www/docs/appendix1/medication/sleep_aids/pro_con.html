Pros and Cons

mTBI

Pro: May help to re-regulate sleep cycle

Con:May worsen dizziness, concentration, memory

Headache

Pro: May help to re-regulate sleep cycle

Con: No additional

Acute Stress

Pro: Probably help with decrease arousal allowing sleep

Con: No additional

PTSD

Pro: Probably help with decrease arousal allowing sleep

Con: No additional

Depression

Pro: Useful in targeting symptoms of sleep, during initial phase of titration with SSRI

Con: May mask one of the target symptoms of SSRI, which may make it difficult to assess the effectiveness of SSRI

Chronic Pain

Pro: No additional

Con: May increase the sedative effect of Opioids
Potential additive effect on respiratory depression

Substance Use Disorder

Pro: No additional

Con: Jeopardize sobriety
Can be habit forming
