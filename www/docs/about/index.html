This application is provided by the TBI Cinical Standards of Care Directorate in partnership with the National Center for Telehealth and Technology(T2). The mTBI Co-Occurring Conditions Toolkit provides guidance to primary care providers on the assessment and management of patients and synthesizes information from the following VA/DoD CPGS: mTBI, PTSD, Depression, Chronic Opioid Therapy and Substance Use Disorder.
