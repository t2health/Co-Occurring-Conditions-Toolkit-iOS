Attention - Acute Stress Disorder

Attention Symptoms

  • Re-experiences intrusive memories
  • Emotional numbing
  • Difficulty multitasking
  • Worsens with emotional distress
  • Dissociative episodes

Tools

  • PC-PTSD
  • PHQ-2
  • Assess for quality of sleep and significant snoring

Action Recommended

  • If PC-PTSD is positive on >2 items, administer Acute Stress Disorder (ASD) Scale to further assess for possible ASD
  • If either question in PHQ-2 scores >2, administer PHQ-9 to further assess for possible depression
  • Consider sleep questionnaire such as the PSQI
