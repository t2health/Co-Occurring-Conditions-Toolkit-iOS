Sleep - Depression

Sleep Symptoms

  • Difficulty falling asleep due to ruminations
  • Early AM/nighttime awakening (unexplained)

Tools

  • PHQ-2
  • Assess for quality of sleep and significant snoring

Action Recommended

  • If either question in PHQ-2 scores >2, administer PHQ-9 to further assess for possible depression
  • Consider sleep questionnaire such as the PSQI
