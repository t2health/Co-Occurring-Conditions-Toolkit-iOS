Treatment Options

First Steps

Initial treatment approach for all conditions includes ruling out medication side effects as well as other diagnoses such as sleep apnea. Utilization of assessment tools may assist with this. Education of diagnosis as well as an emphasis on sleep hygiene, relaxation techniques and correction of circadian rhythm disruptions from travel are important initial steps.

  • Rule out primary sleep disorder as contributing to mood disturbance, and treat if indicated
  • Consider watchful waiting vs. medication for depression vs. referral for treatment of depression
  • Medications to consider for the treatment of depression and/or referral for psychotherapy:
    • SSRIs
    • Serotonin/Norepinephrine Antagonist
    • Norepinephrine/Dopamine Re-uptake Inhibitor
    • Serotonin/Norepinephrine Re-uptake Inhibitor

Second Steps

Reassess for efficacy of initial intervention utilizing the same tools from First Steps.

  • Consider follow up in 3-4 weeks, sooner if clinically indicated
  • Discuss treatment options and patient's preference
  • Consider referral to Behavioral Health for treatment of depression
