Sleep - PTSD

Sleep Symptoms

  • Fear of sleep due to nightmares
  • Difficulty falling asleep due to ruminations
  • Early AM/nighttime awakening (unexplained)

Tools

  • PC-PTSD
  • Consider AUDIT-C and investigation of substance use given frequent co-occurrence
  • Assess for quality of sleep and significant snoring

Action Recommended

  • If PC-PTSD is positive on >2 items, administer PCL-M to further assess for possible PTSD
  • If AUDIT-C ≥3 (F), ≥4 (M), then consider referral to Behavioral Health vs. education depending on symptom severity
  • Consider DAST-20 if suspicion of other substance use
  • Consider sleep questionnaire such as the PSQI
