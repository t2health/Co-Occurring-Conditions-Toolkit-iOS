Purpose

The purpose of this guide is to help the primary care provider in the non-deployed setting initiate appropriate symptom management when multiple diagnoses may be present, either resulting in resolution of symptoms or while awaiting specialty appointments. This guide does not replace clinical judgment or specialty consultation.

How to Use this Guide

  1. Identify and select target symptom (sleep, mood, attention, chronic pain).
  2. Identify the characteristics of the target symptom
  3. Based on the selected characteristics of the target symptom, the probable etiology(ies) will appear
  4. Consider the assessment tools of each potential co-occurring disorder to assist with assessment and outcome monitoring.
  5. Consider ensuing action recommended.
  6. Consider initial treatment steps, as well as recommended follow up interval and treatment next steps.
  7. If medications are considered, go to Medications under Provider Resources to get information regarding potential effect of certain drugs on potential co-occurring disorders.
  8. Patient Education under Provider Resources contains patient education websites.
  9. Provider Tools contains provider resources, to include assessment tools and screening instruments.
